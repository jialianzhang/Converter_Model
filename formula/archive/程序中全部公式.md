## 五、Step 4 — 耦合反应求解器（核心修复区）

| 编号 | 公式名称 | LaTeX 公式 | 说明 |
|------|---------|-----------|------|
| 4-1 | 各元素氧化n值 | $\begin{aligned} &n_{Si}{=}2, n_{Mn}{=}1, n_P{=}2.5, \\ &n_V{=}1.5, n_{Ti}{=}2, n_{Fe}{=}1, n_C{=}1 \end{aligned}$ | V2按标准氧化态修正 |
| 4-2 | 传质通量 (式2) | $J_M = F_M \{ w([M])^b - w([M])^* \}$ | [mol/(s·m²)] |
| 4-3 | 脱碳通量 (式5) | $J_C = F_C \{ w([C])^b - w([C])^* \} = G_{CO} (P_{CO}^* - 1)$ | |
| 4-4 | CO生成速率 | $G_{CO} = G_{CO}^0 \times (1 + 3q_{CO}/q_{Ar})$ | GCO0=80(工业直标) |
| 4-5 | 界面氧守恒 (式6) | $2J_{Si} + J_{Mn} + 2.5J_P + 1.5J_V + 2J_{Ti} + J_{Fe} + J_C - J_O = 0$ | Brent求解a*O |
| **4-6** | **O₂注入限制（核心修复）** | $J_{O2}^{inj} = \dfrac{O_{2,mol/s} \times 2}{A} \times 0.05 \times f_{O2}$ | **$f_{O2} = \max(0.04,\ w_C/0.8)$，C<1%时线性降氧** |
| 4-7 | 渣侧氧势 | $a_O^b = \dfrac{w(FeO) \cdot f_{FeO}}{K_{Fe}}$ | |
| **4-8** | **渣侧氧势C联动上限（核心修复）** | $a_O^b \leq \begin{cases} 1.5, & w_C>0.5\% \\ \max(0.3,\ 1.5 \cdot w_C/0.5), & w_C\leq0.5\% \end{cases}$ | **C=0.04%→cap=0.12** |
| 4-9 | FeO反馈 | $a_O^b \leftarrow a_O^b \times \max(0.15,\ 1 - (w_{FeO}-25) \times 0.04)$；w_FeO>25%触发 | |
| 4-10 | 界面浓度通用 (式7) | $w([M])^* = \dfrac{F_{MO_n} \cdot w((MO_n))^b + F_M \cdot w([M])^b}{F_M + F_{MO_n} \cdot E_M \cdot (a_O^*)^n}$ | Si,Mn,P,V,Ti,Fe |
| 4-11 | 碳专属界面浓度 | $w([C])^* = \dfrac{F_C \cdot w([C])^b + G_{CO}}{F_C + G_{CO} \cdot K_C \cdot a_O^*}$ | CO为气体产物 |
| 4-12 | 反应速率 (式8) | $-\dfrac{\Delta m_i}{\Delta t} = A \cdot F_i \cdot M_i \{ w([i])^b - w([i])^* \}$ | Si/Mn/P: $w_b{>}0.01\%\to w^*{\leq}w_b{\times}0.5$ |
